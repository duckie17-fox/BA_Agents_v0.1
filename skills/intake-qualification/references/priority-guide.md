# Priority Guide

## P0 — Critical (làm ngay)
Any of:
- Customer threatening to churn or escalating to leadership
- Revenue-blocking issue (cannot close deal / cannot invoice)
- Production bug affecting core workflow
- Compliance or legal deadline

## P1 — High (sprint này hoặc sprint tới)
Any of:
- Frequently requested by multiple customers (3+)
- Blocks a significant workflow but workaround exists
- Supports a committed roadmap item
- Raised by Sales for active deals

## P2 — Normal (backlog, prioritize trong planning)
- Nice-to-have improvement
- Single customer request without urgency signals
- Internal efficiency gain
- Enhancement to existing feature

**Signals to watch for:**
- "khách hàng nói sẽ hủy hợp đồng" → P0
- "deal $X đang chờ feature này" → P0 or P1
- "nhiều khách hỏi về" → P1
- "sẽ tốt hơn nếu có" → P2
